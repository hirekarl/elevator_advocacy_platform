## Description

```
Is your elevator working today?

For millions of New York City tenants — especially seniors and residents with disabilities — a broken elevator isn't an inconvenience. It's a crisis. In 2025, a Bronx resident passed away during a heat wave, unable to leave their 4th-floor apartment during an extended elevator outage. In Coney Island, one building logged 47 outages in a single year. In Queens, a 100-year-old woman was trapped in her home for over 30 days.

Elevator Advocate is a free tenant advocacy platform that turns elevator outage data into a tool for action. Search your building's address, see your full complaint history pulled directly from NYC Open Data, flag outages in real time, and get an AI-generated 311 script with exactly what to say when you call.

🔗 Try it live → https://elevatoradvocate.nyc
💻 Open source → https://github.com/hirekarl/elevator_advocate

Built as an AI-Native MVP during the Pursuit Fellowship (April 2026) — from git init to deployed platform in 5 days — using a multi-agent development system: Django 6.0, React 19, NYC Planning GeoSearch, NYC Open Data SODA API, and Google Gemini 2.5 Flash.
```
